# Phase 2 — Image CNNs

**Weeks 4–7** — Train and fine-tune image classifiers. Build a `cnn_kramer` prototype.

## Goal

Be able to take a pretrained CNN, fine-tune it on a new domain, and properly evaluate it. This single technique transfers across many image-based clinical ML problems.

## Key references
- fast.ai *Practical Deep Learning for Coders*, Lessons 1–4
- He et al. 2015 (ResNet)
- Esteva et al. 2017 (Nature dermatology paper — the medical CV transfer learning benchmark)
